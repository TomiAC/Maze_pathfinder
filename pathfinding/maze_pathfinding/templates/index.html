Maze Pathfinder

The website is an interactive platform designed to test and visualize the functioning of different search algorithms applied to maze solving. The available algorithms include A* (A star), Dijkstra, Breadth-First Search (BFS), and Depth-First Search (DFS).

Things to keep in mind

  • 😀 is the Start
  • 🏁 is the Finish
  • 🌳 are prohibited cells
  • 🔎 are paths followed
  • 🚶 are the cells that make the path to the finish
